About My Bachelor's Degree

I earned my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the Federal University of Espírito Santo (UFES) between 2008 and 2014. This program provided me with a strong foundation in both theoretical and practical aspects of computing.

Key Disciplines

  • Algorithms and Data Structures
  • Operating Systems
  • Computer Networks
  • Database Systems
  • Software Engineering
  • Computer Graphics
  • Structured Systems Design and Analysis

These courses were part of the 1993 curriculum, which emphasized a balance between theoretical knowledge and practical skills, preparing students for diverse challenges in the field of computer science.

Participation in NINFA Laboratory

During my undergraduate studies, I was an active participant in the NINFA Laboratory. This experience allowed me to engage in research and development activities, enhancing my skills in problem-solving, teamwork, and innovation.

Graduate Profile

As a graduate of this program, I was trained to design, develop, and manage computational systems, applying innovative solutions to real-world problems. The program emphasized ethical responsibility, teamwork, and continuous learning, enabling me to adapt to the ever-evolving technology landscape.
