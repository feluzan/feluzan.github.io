During my Master's program, I was a member of the NEMO Laboratory, under the supervision of Professor Vítor Souza.

My research resulted in the dissertation titled "Ontological Representation of Object-Relational Mapping Frameworks" (2020) .

Additionally, I published a paper titled "Ontological Analysis of Object-Relational Mapping Frameworks" , which was presented at the Brazilian Seminar on Ontology Research (ONTOBRAS) in 2019.
